Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about SurePath School

What are your admission requirements?

We welcome students of all backgrounds. Requirements include completed application, academic records, and an assessment appropriate for the student's grade level.

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes, we offer need-based financial assistance and merit scholarships. Contact our admissions office for more information about available programs.

What is your student-teacher ratio?

We maintain small class sizes with an average student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention and optimal learning outcomes.

How do I order school supplies?

You can order supplies through our online school shop. We offer convenient delivery options and bulk discounts for families with multiple children.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We offer over 30 clubs and activities including sports, arts, academic competitions, community service, and leadership programs.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we offer bus transportation to most areas within a 15-mile radius of the school. Route information is available upon enrollment.
